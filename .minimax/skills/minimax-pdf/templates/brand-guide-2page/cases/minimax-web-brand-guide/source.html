06 / Do's & Don'ts

Do

✓Use the gradient sparingly — reserve Magenta to Coral for hero moments, CTA accents and generative motion cues.
✓Keep modules distinct — text, speech, video and music can each own a card, but typography should remain consistent.
✓Show product proof — name models, versions, APIs and outcomes when making capability claims.
✓Leave generous whitespace — let the dark wordmark and bright swatches carry the identity.

Don't

×Do not recolor the wordmark — keep it Ink Core or white on dark backgrounds.
×Do not stack all accent colors — choose one dominant accent per composition.
×Do not overuse generic AI motifs — avoid random sparkles, robot icons and ungrounded purple haze.
×Do not bury CTAs — API, Agent and Hailuo links should remain direct and action-oriented.
